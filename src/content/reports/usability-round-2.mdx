<ReportSection number="01" title="Context" description="Following Round 1 (Nov 2023), a prototype was built to address the primary blockers found — search/transfer confusion, error handling, and discoverability. Round 2 tests whether those fixes worked.">

<Callout title="What changed between rounds:">

Round 1 revealed that users couldn't distinguish between search and transfer in the homepage search bar. The redesigned prototype separated these flows, introduced a clearer modal structure, and added a bulk transfer option with CSV upload. Round 2 tested this prototype with 4 participants — same 3 tasks.

<Annotation number="1" title="Search/transfer confusion — resolved." text="The #1 issue from Round 1 ('Search or transfer a domain' placeholder confused all 3 participants) did not resurface. The redesigned modal with separated flows eliminated the ambiguity. All 4 participants found domain search straightforward." />

<Annotation number="2" title="Bulk transfer entry point — still a problem." text="Round 1 flagged discoverability (issue #4: 'Transfer section is hard to find'). Round 2 confirms this from a different angle: 3 of 4 users went to the menu first, not the search bar. The search bar works — but users don't expect transfers to live there." />

<Annotation number="3" title="Error handling — improved but EPP codes now the gap." text="Round 1's error handling issues (silent failures, unclear format guidance) were not reproduced. <ReportSection number="05" title="Findings" description="Answering the 3 test goals set before the study.">

<Callout title="1. Have we resolved the search modal misunderstandings?">

Yes. The main usability issues from Round 1 were fixed. One user was unfamiliar with "bulk" as a concept, but the modal components themselves were clear.

</Callout>

<Callout title="2. Is the search bar the primary option for initiating a transfer?">

No — not intuitively. Some users overlook the "search or transfer" placeholder or assume transfers belong in the menu. The search bar works once found, but discoverability needs a secondary entry point (menu or domains page).

</Callout>

<Callout title="3. Can users complete all flows without difficulty?">

Yes — all users completed all tasks. The only friction was finding the transfer entry point and the invisible EPP tooltip. Once inside the flow, the experience was rated Easy to Very Easy across the board.

</Callout>
