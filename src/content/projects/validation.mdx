<PostHeader title="Validation—identity verification, designed from scratch">
  <p>Validation.com was an identity verification platform built under the Namecheap umbrella. The idea was simple: let businesses verify customers online the way you'd do it in person—selfie plus ID card, reviewed side-by-side. No facial recognition, no AI black box. Human judgment, made easy. I was the sole designer—responsible for everything from research through to the shipped product and marketing site.</p>
</PostHeader>
