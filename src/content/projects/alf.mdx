We designed every interaction from scratch: how the assistant speaks, how it handles what it can't do, how commerce works inside chat, and how it all hands off to the browser when it needs to. The kind of project where every edge case teaches you something.</p>
    <p>The app shipped, the App Store assets landed, and the experience holds up. That's the part that matters—it works the way it should, and it feels like someone actually thought about it.</p>
