    <p>Manager applications are where the platform lives—domains, hosting, subscriptions, connections. Each one serves a different function, but they all share the same design language and structural patterns.</p>
    <p>We built a system around this: guidelines, documentation, and integration rules that teams across the company could follow to ship consistent products without losing speed. Getting that right—and earning the trust to make it stick—was some of the most rewarding work I did at Spaceship.</p>

<PostSection media="center" padding="spacing-xl">
  <PostText>
    <p>Unbox is where Spaceship goes from "I bought a domain" to "I have a working site." It's the post-purchase flow that guides users through connecting and configuring everything — hosting, email, SSL, CMS — into one setup. The design challenge was real: dozens of product combinations, each with its own logic, all needing to feel like one path. We mapped 12+ scenarios and designed a system that absorbs the complexity underneath. Choose, connect, configure, create.</p>
  </PostText>
