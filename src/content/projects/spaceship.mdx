      <h2 class="post-text__title">Summary</h2>
      <p>Spaceship is a Namecheap spin-off built to reimagine the online presence industry from scratch. I joined before there was a product. Just a vision and a blank slate. Over six years, that turned into a fully integrated platform covering domains, hosting, email, DNS, security, and more. Everything shown here—the homepage, the search component, the manager applications, Unbox—is work I contributed to as part of the Product Platform UX team, from the first concepts to production.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="post-text">
      <h2 class="post-text__title">Impact</h2>
      <p>The platform reached <strong>5 million domains under management</strong>, 86% of which are new registrations—people choosing Spaceship, not just migrating to it. It holds a <strong>4.4★ rating on Trustpilot</strong> across 4,000+ reviews, with the interface and ease of use consistently highlighted as what sets it apart.</p>
