      <h2 class="post-text__title">About</h2>
      <p><a href="https://www.spaceship.com">Spaceship</a> is a Namecheap spin-off built to reimagine the online presence industry from scratch. I joined before there was a product. Just a vision and a blank slate. Over six years, that turned into a fully integrated platform covering domains, hosting, email, DNS, security, and more. Everything shown here—the homepage, the search component, the manager applications, Unbox—is work I contributed to as part of the Product Platform UX team, from the first concepts to production.</p>

      <h2 class="post-text__title">Impact</h2>
