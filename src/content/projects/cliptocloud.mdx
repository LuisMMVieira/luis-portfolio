<PostHeader title="A clipboard manager we designed and shipped ourselves">
  <p>ClipToCloud started as a conversation between me and my friend Miguel. We both relied on clipboard managers daily and kept running into the same frustrations—no sync, no folders, no search. So we built our own. I handled everything visual—brand, product UI, marketing website, App Store presence. Miguel wrote all the Swift. We shipped it to the Mac App Store and iOS App Store without a team, a budget, or a deadline.</p>
</PostHeader>

No accounts required, end-to-end encrypted. It shipped to both App Stores and had a marketing website. The project is no longer active, but the work stands as proof that when something bothers me enough, I'll design and ship it myself.</p>
