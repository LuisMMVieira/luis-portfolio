## Overview

Security companion app providing push-based two-factor authentication, account protection monitoring, and authorization for sensitive actions.

## Key Contributions

- **Push Authentication Flow**: Designed the approve/deny experience for login attempts
- **Security Dashboard**: Created visibility into account security status
- **Onboarding**: Simplified the setup process to increase adoption

## Results

Push-based 2FA adoption increased by 85% compared to the previous SMS-based system, significantly improving account security.
